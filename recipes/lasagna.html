Lasagna

Description

Home

Lasagna is a baked Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is warm, filling, and perfect for dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, then let it simmer.
  3. Spread some sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
  5. Add meat sauce and cheese on top.
  6. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  7. Cover with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
