Burger

Description

Home

A burger is a popular meal made with a cooked patty inside a bun, usually served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and sauce. It is quick, filling, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef patty
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup or burger sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Season the beef patty with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patty in a pan until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add cheese on top and let it melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Add sauce, lettuce, tomato, onion, and the patty to the bun.
  6. Close the burger and serve warm.
